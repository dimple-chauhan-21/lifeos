## Purpose

Sprint 02 applies the templates approved in Sprint 01 to every screen in the Screen Inventory (`docs/product/06_Screen_Inventory.md` — ~39 unique screens after `docs/decisions/DEC-012`). Wireframes here are structural and grayscale: layout, hierarchy, and content placement only — no color, typography, or component styling, which belong to Sprint 03.

Wireframe the six reusable templates first (Entity Form, Entity Overview, Capability Tab Shell, Filterable List Shell, Confirm Action, Attachment/File Viewer), validated against at least two structurally different Entity Types (Vehicle and Contact, per `docs/design/00_Design_Handoff.md`, Section 12) before wireframing any one-off screen (Dashboard Home, Settings, Auth screens).

## What Belongs in Each Folder

| Folder | Contents |

| `review/` | Sign-off notes, feedback, and the record of Product Owner approval for this sprint's deliverables before Sprint 03 begins |

## Exit Criteria

Every screen in the Screen Inventory has a corresponding wireframe, and all open UX questions that affect layout (e.g., the mobile tab-collapse behavior, UX-010) have been resolved in practice, not just in principle. Reviewed and accepted, per `PROJECT_STATUS.md`, before Sprint 03 begins.
